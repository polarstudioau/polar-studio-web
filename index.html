Technical drafting & BIM answers

Clear, technical answers regarding drafting processes, compliance standards, file delivery, and project onboarding for Australian construction and infrastructure.

Turnaround times depend on drawing complexity. Standard markup updates (redlines) are typically turned around quickly. Creating entirely new drawings from scratch involves a longer lead time to ensure accuracy. Precise timeframes are always provided upfront upon reviewing the project scope. For urgent deadlines, priority processing is available.

We also hold official BIM ISO 19650 registration, guaranteeing that model management and coordination strictly comply with the highest international protocols and Australian Tier 1 requirements. Documentation is produced in accordance with the NCC (National Construction Code) and our Revit models align with NATSPEC guidelines.
Standard deliverables include fully updated native source files (Revit .rvt and AutoCAD .dwg), alongside clean, high-resolution PDF sets. We accept DWG, RVT, PDF, and most standard CAD/BIM formats. Workflows can be adapted to accommodate specific software environments with prior notice, ensuring seamless integration with your project standards.
