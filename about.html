About Polar Studio

Precision drafting
for Australia's AEC leaders

Polar Studio is driven by a team of experienced architects and engineers. We deliver BIM, technical drafting and As-Built documentation to the standards that define the Australian construction industry.

Our Story

Built on 7 years of hands-on AEC experience

Polar Studio was born from over 7 years of experience working within a major world-renowned Australian architecture and engineering firm. That background gave us a deep understanding of what project teams really need from their drafting and BIM partners: accuracy, speed, and an obsession with standards.

Today, we deliver technical documentation across infrastructure, commercial, residential, industrial, and real estate sectors throughout Australia — from Sydney to Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide. Every deliverable is produced strictly to AS 1100 and ISO 19650, with LOD 500 detail for models that need to last the lifetime of the asset.

We specialise in workshop-ready documentation: the kind of drawings that actually get built, fabricated, and approved first time — no back-and-forth, no revisions over detail that should have been captured from day one.
